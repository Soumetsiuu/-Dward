About Me, Edward.

Hey there! I'm Edward, a grade 12 student on the ICT track. My love affair with technology started young, like, "exploratory surgery on mom's new tablet" young. Buttons, settings, apps – I went in there like a tech-crazed explorer, leaving a trail of frozen screens in my wake.


As I grew, my tinkering spread to household appliances. Toasters, TVs – nothing was safe from my screwdriver (and sometimes not-so-safe attempts). But hey, you gotta break a few eggs (or maybe circuit boards) to make an omelet (or, you know, fix things!). Now, I can build a PC from scratch, picking the perfect parts like a pro treasure hunter.


Those childhood mishaps, believe it or not, were my stepping stones to success. Every "oops" taught me how electronics work, and each fix fueled my passion. Now, I'm on a quest to become a programmer, then a computer engineer, to truly understand the magic behind the gadgets we love.


Technology has always been my playground, and now I'm building something awesome: my future. It wasn't always smooth sailing (hello, thousand-death laptop!), but the journey has been epic, filled with tinkering, triumphs, and the thrill of making something work that "shouldn't." And that's why I'm diving headfirst into the world of ICT, even if math gives me a headache. My passion for tech burns brighter than any soldering iron, and I'm ready to code, build, and explore the ever-evolving world of technology.
